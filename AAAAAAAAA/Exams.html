GATE
Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an examination that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of various undergraduate subjects in engineering and science for admission into the Masters Program and Job in Public Sector Companies.

Frequency: Annual
Placeholder

ESE(IES)
Engineering Services Examination commonly known as ESE is conducted annually by UPSC to recruit engineers in four domains namely CE,ME,EE & E&T for the techno managerial posts. The examination constitutes a two-stage written examination followed by an interview.

Frequency: Annual
Placeholder

DRDO
The Defence Research and Development Organisation is an agency under the Department of Defence Research and Development in Ministry of Defence of the Government of India, charged with the military's research and development, headquartered in Delhi, India.

Frequency: Annual
Placeholder

ISRO
With a total of 32 centers all over India, every year ISRO entrance exam is conducted for which candidates are invited from Civil, Electrical, Architecture, Mechanical, Electronics, computer science and Refrigeration and Air Conditioning streams.

Frequency: Annual
Placeholder

UPPSC AE
UPPSC stands for Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission. This is a government established that aims at providing state-level government jobs to the youth of Uttar Pradesh. UPPSC AE is a recruitment examination conducted for the recruitment of eligible and able candidates for the post of AE.

Frequency: Annual
Placeholder

SSC JE
Staff Selection Commission (SSC) conducts the SSC JE Exam every year to select eligible candidates for the recruitment of Junior Engineers (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, and Quantity Surveying and Contracts) posts for various Departments/ Organizations in the Government of India.

Frequency: Annual
