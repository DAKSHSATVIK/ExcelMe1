Yearwise CAT Cutoff



CAT Cutoff 2021:

CAT 2021 cut off will be released by various IIMs on its official website. The minimum marks required for the examinees to get a call for the MBA selection process are called CAT Cutoff 2021. The CAT Cutoff for IIM is typically up to 99 to 100 percentile for general category students. For the reserved category students, the cutoff of CAT 2021 is slightly lower at 97 to 98 percentile. The expected cutoff for CAT 2021 to enroll in top MBA colleges like SPJIMR, FMS, MDI,etc may be 95 to 99 percentile.

Factors Determining CAT Cut off 2021

Before releasing the IIM CAT 2021 cutoff, institutes will take into account certain factors. Listed below are some of the key factors considered by IIMs to have been revealed in the previous year’s CAT cutoff.
