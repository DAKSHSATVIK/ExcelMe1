Exam Pattern



The examination shall be conducted according to the following plan:—

The Exam Pattern for the UPPSC AE consists of Two Written Papers(Paper I and PaperII) and an interview. A total of 250 questions are asked in both the papers combined. The papers are conducted for 150minutes each. It will be conducted through offline.


  • The written test consists of different sections.
  • The exam consists of only multiple choice.
  • Each section has different marks.
  • Each section contains a different weightage.

Marking Scheme(Both Paper-I And Paper-II):-

  • Correct answer: +3 marks
  • Incorrect answer: -0.33 marks
  • Unanswered questions: 0 marks


Papers Number of Questions Maximum Marks Duration of Exam

Interview - 100

UPPSC AE Exam Pattern Paper-I:-


The paper will consist of General Hindi and Concerned Subject-I(Mechanical/Civil/ Electrical/ Agriculture Engineering).


  • It will be conducted through offline mode.
  • total number of questions is 125
  • The time duration for paper-I is 150minutes.
  • Questions will be objective type.
  • The exam will be conducted for 375 marks.

Papers Number Of Question Maxmium Marks Duration Of Exam
Paper 1 (General Hindi) 25 75 150minutes
Paper 1 (Mains Subjects (Electrical/ Civil/ Mechanical/ Agriculture)) 100 300
Total 125 375 marks

UPPSC AE Exam Pattern Paper-II:-


Paper-II is the secondary level of the selection process. The paper will consist of General Studies and Concerned Subject-I(Mechanical/Civil/ Electrical/ Agriculture Engineering).


  • It will be conducted through offline mode.
  • There will be 125 questions asked in the examination.
  • The time duration for paper-II is 150 minutes.
  • Questions will be objective type.
  • The exam will be conducted for 375 marks.
Papers Number of Questions Maximum Marks Duration of Exam
Paper II (General Studies) 25 75 150 minutes
Paper II (Mains Subjects (Electrical/ Civil/ Mechanical/ Agriculture)) 100 Questions 300
Total 125 Questions 375 marks


UPPSC AE Interview:-


The Interview is the final stage of the process. The selected candidates will have to download the Interview letter. The interview will be conducted for 100 marks. The selection will be made on the basis of total marks obtained by the candidates in written examination and interview.

It is the final stage in IES recruitment exam.
