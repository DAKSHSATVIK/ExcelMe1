Exam Pattern



CAT 2021 Exam Pattern

Particulars Details
Mode of Exam Computer Based Examination
Exam Duration 180 Minutes [3 Hours]
Question Type Objective (MCQ Based) and Subjective
Total Questions 100
Total Marks 300
Sections Verbal & Reading Comprehension, Quantitative Ability, Data adequacy and logical reasoning
Marks Awarded +3 Marks for every correct answer
Marking Scheme -1 Marks for every wrong answer in objective questions and No Negative Marking for subjective questions

*CAT 2020 exam duration was reduced to 2 hours due to COVID-19 pandemic. Final allotted time for the exam will be announced through official notification.



Here is the section wise distribution of marks for CAT 2021 -

Subject Marks Allotted Number of Questions
Verbal & Reading Comprehension 102 34
Quantitative Ability 96 32
Data adequacy and logical reasoning 102 34
Total 300 100
