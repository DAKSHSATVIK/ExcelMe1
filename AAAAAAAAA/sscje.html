What's SSC-JE Exam ?


The Staff Selection Commission holds an open competitive Examination for recruitment of Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Quantity Surveying & Contracts) for various Ministries/ Departments/ Organizations in the Government of India. The posts are Group – B (Non-Gazetted) in level-6 (Rs 35,400-1,12,400) of pay matrix of the seventh Central Pay Commission. Those Candidates who are interested in the vacancy details & completed all eligibility criteria can read the Notification & Apply Online.
Post : Junior Engineer

Various posts through SSC-JE Exams are :

S.NO Organization Post
1 Border Road Organization (BRO) JE (Civil)
2 Border Road Organization (BRO) JE(Electrical & Mechanical)
3 Central Public Works Department (CPWD) JE (Civil)
4 Central Public Works Department (CPWD) JE(Electrical)
5 Central Water and Power Research Station JE (Civil)
6 Central Water and Power Research Station JE (Electrical)
7 Central Water and Power Research Station JE (Mechanical)
8 Central Water Commission JE (Civil)
9 Central Water Commission JE (Mechanical)
10 Directorate of Quality Assurance (Naval) JE (Mechanical)

 

Eligibility :


Nationality/Citizenship :

A candidate must be either :

  • A citizen of India, or

  • A subject of Nepal/Bhutan, or

  • A Tibetan refugee who came over to India before 1st January, 1962 with the intention of permanently settling in India, or

  • A person of Indian origin migrated from Pakistan, Burma, Sri Lanka and East African countries of Kenya, Uganda, the United Republic of Tanzania, Zambia, Malawi, Zaire, Ethiopia and Vietnam with the intention of permanently setting in India

Total Questions 65

Sections Aptitude Engineering, Mathematics, Subject-specific questions
